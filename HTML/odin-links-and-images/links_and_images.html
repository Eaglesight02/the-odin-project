Attributes:

  • href: it gives the uri (either local or in the world wide web) to point to:

    • Sections of a page with document fragments
    • Specific text portions with text fragments
    • Pieces of media files with media fragments
    • Telephone numbers with tel: URLs
    • Email addresses with maito: URLs
    • SMS text messages with sms: URLs

  • target: where the given href should be opened:

    • _blank: a new tab or window(needs to be customized).
    • _self: in the current session or frame or context.
    • _parent: in the parent frame or context
    • _top: in the top level frame or context

  • rel: The relationship of the linked URL as a space-separated link types.

    • noreferer: This would not send the referer header. The referer headers contains information about the page that requested the URL, mostly used for user-analytics and logging.
